Who We Are

Embark on a thrilling journey with AquaMetrics Oceanography, the trailblazers in oceanographic solutions worldwide.

From unravelling the mysteries of water turbidity to delving into the depths of marine and geological studies, we redefine excellence in ocean exploration.

Join us in safeguarding marine resources and exploring the uncharted territories of the ocean with cutting-edge technology and unwavering dedication.

Services

Water Quality and Ecosystem Monitoring Turbidity Monitoring: Measurement and analysis of water turbidity using turbidity profilers. Price: $500 per study (up to 10 samples). Water Quality Evaluation: Analysis of biological water parameters including nutrients, dissolved oxygen, and bacteriology. Price: $800 per study (up to 10 samples). Phytoplankton and Chlorophyll Analysis: Monitoring of phytoplankton and chlorophyll concentrations using optical sensors and fluorometers. Price: $400 per study (up to 10 samples). Dissolved Organic Carbon Analysis: Measurement of dissolved organic carbon in marine water to assess water quality and ecosystem health. Price: $900 per study (up to 10 samples). Ocean Acidification Monitoring: Measurement of pH and alkalinity to assess ocean acidification. Price: $1,000 per study (up to 10 samples). Coastal Ecosystems Integrated Monitoring: Comprehensive evaluation of coastal ecosystems, including water quality, biodiversity, and marine currents. Price: $5,000 per study (up to 10 kilometers of coast). Marine Mapping and Geological Studies Marine Bottom Cartography: Detailed mapping of the sea floor using multispectral cameras and side-scan sonar. Price: $1,500 per square kilometer. Marine Habitats Mapping: Identification and mapping of marine habitats using multispectral cameras and remote sensing techniques. Price: $1,000 per square kilometer. Submarine Geological Cartography: Geological mapping of the sea floor using sonar techniques and sediment sampling. Price: $1,500 per square kilometer. Marine Geological Risk Evaluation: Identification and analysis of geological risks in marine areas. Price: $2,000 per study. Marine Sediments Analysis: Collection and analysis of marine sediments to determine composition, origin, and formation processes. Price: $1,000 per study (up to 5 samples). Physical Oceanography and Structural Monitoring Temperature and Salinity Profiles: Profiling of temperature and salinity at various depths using CTD profilers. Price: $700 per profile (up to 200 meters deep). Marine Currents Studies: Monitoring and analysis of marine currents using current profilers. Price: $1,000 per week. Waves and Tides Analysis: Recording and analysis of wave and tide data using pressure sensors and wave buoys. Price: $1,200 per month. Coastal Erosion Studies: Evaluation and monitoring of coastal erosion using drones and remote sensing techniques. Price: $2,000 per study (up to 5 kilometers of coast). Submarine Structures Inspection: Monitoring and inspection of docks, anchored structures, and piles using underwater drones with cameras. Price: $1,800 per inspection (up to 1 kilometer of structures). Marinas and Ports Monitoring: Assessment of physical conditions and water quality in marinas and ports using water quality sensors and underwater drones. Price: $2,500 per study. Run-Up Studies: Evaluation of flooding potential due to storm surges, tsunamis, or sea-level rise. Price: $2,500 per study (up to coast). Environmental Impact and Special Studies Marine Pollution Studies: Evaluation of chemical pollutants in water and marine sediments, including heavy metals and hydrocarbons. Price: $1,200 per study (up to 10 samples). Marine Environmental Impact Studies: Analysis of environmental impacts of coastal and marine projects, including physical and geological evaluations. Price: $3,000 per study. Sargassum Monitoring and Management: Detection and quantification of sargassum in coastal areas using remote sensing images and multispectral cameras. Price: $600 per study (up to 5 kilometers of coast). Sargassum Detection and Management Strategy: Monitoring and planning of sargassum management strategies in affected areas. Price: $1,500 per study (up to 5 kilometers of coast).
